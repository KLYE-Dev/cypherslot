Golden Faucet Help

Golden Faucet is a radically different type of slot game in which you pick the results and where you can win real cryptocurrency for free!

Unlike traditional slot games, credits for Golden Faucet are earned by interacting with cryptocurrency faucets (hence the name).

You can quickly earn enough credits to play for a chance to win real cryptocurrency. When you run out of credits simply replenish them - you never need to wait for a faucet's time limit to elapse and your account is credited instantly!

Winning prizes are sent to the payout address you specify immediately - no need to wait for some minimum value or time limit to elapse.

Even better, each spin can earn you shares in the game. Once you've achieved the status of shareholder you become eligible to receive a percentage of the game's profits, paid out at regular intervals. Even when you don't win, you win!


Earning Credits


One spin (1 credit), in Golden Faucet costs 1,000 gwei.

When you first start the game you will be asked to enter a valid cryptocurrency address at the bottom of the game area. Each address has its own balance which is displayed in the "Balance" field.

You can change this payout address at any time by clicking on the change accounts button.

To add credits to the payout address click on the add funds button. An faucet browser window will pop up with a special, pre-filled wallet address (don't change this!). Simply verify that you're a human and click on the appropriate buttons to claim the faucet's random credits. The faucet window will automatically close and your balance will be credited.

If you change the special, pre-filled account address in the faucet browser window your account will not be credited!

How To Play

Click on the spin button to start the reels spinning. If your account balance is too low a faucet window will automatically pop up.

Three selector areas will appear at the right side of the game. Each selector contains encrypted values, presented as coloured squares, representing stop positions on the reels. The top selector contains stop positions for the left reel, the middle selector contains stop positions for the middle reel, and the bottom selector contains stop positions for the right reel.

Rolling over the coloured squares will reveal the encrypted values they represent. You may choose one encrypted stop position per reel. When the game is completed you can verify that the encrypted stop positions you chose for each reel match the final results.

Click on the submit button to send your selections to the game host. The game host will reply with the encryption key which will be used to determine which stop positions you've chosen and the reels will stop at the appropriate positions. Any wins or shares awards will be displayed when all reels have stoppped spinning.

A paytable will appear on the right side of the game displaying the possible winning combinations on the payline (arrow). You can dismiss the paytable by clicking anywhere on the game screen.

To play the game in a traditional slots way, enable the "Auto-select results setting. When you click the Spin button your computer will randomly select the encrypted reel stop positions for you.

Winning

Winning combinations are those that appear only on the payline - the middle line of icons pointed to by the paytable.

Payout combinations - those that immediately pay cryptocurrency to the specified account - must appear on the payline of all three reels (x3). A winning payout combination is sent to the associated cryptocurrency account immediately and should appear in the account's balance when the next block is mined.

Shares combinations - those that award shares in the game's profits - may appear on the payline of any reel (x1). Only the highest shares combination will win. For example, if a Guy Fawkes icon appears on reel 1 and a bars icon appears on reel 3, only the Guy Fawkes combination will win and the total prize will be 9 shares. Multiple same winning combinations are counted as one. For example, three Guy Fawkes icons appearing on the payline will win 9 shares.

A player becomes an eligible shareholder when they've reached the threshold number of spins as denoted by the threshold percentage bar on the left side of the game. When this bar is full (100%), the player becomes eligible for a portion of the game's available profits.

The amount that the player becomes eligible for is calculated as player's shares divided by total shares issued multiplied by available profits.

For example, if a shareholder has accumulated 100 shares and 1,000 have been issued in total then the shareholder is eligible to earn 10% of the game's available profits. If 50,000,000 cryptocurrency units are available from game profits, the shareholder will receive 500,000 units.

Shares earnings are issued on a regular basis and the number of shares and threshold percentage are reset to 0 on payout. If the threshold has not been achieved, the number of shares earned is carried over into the next payout period.

Shareholder statistics are available by clicking on the threshold percentage bar.
